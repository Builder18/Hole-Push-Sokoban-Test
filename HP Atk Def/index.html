Player has three buttons: HP, Atk and Def. (Health, Attack and Defence)
Pressing one of buttons increases that stat but you have limited points to assign.
Alternatively you can keep your mouse over the button to auto-buy it.

Defeating enemies gives you stat points.

All enemies have HP of 1. To defeat them, have attack one greater than enemy's Def is. For baddie2, 3 attack is enough.

When stat symbols on top appear, it means that you gain more of that stat when using stat points to buy it.

For HP, it increases every prestige. For Def, it increases to 5. For Atk, it increases to 3.
